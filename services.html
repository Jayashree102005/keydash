FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have my original key?
No. Our technicians can generate a new key for most vehicles using your VIN number or through on-site decoding of your vehicle's lock cylinder. No original key required.
How long does programming take?
Standard transponder keys take 15-30 minutes. Smart fobs and proximity keys typically require 30-60 minutes depending on the vehicle's security system complexity.
Do you service luxury or European vehicles?
Absolutely. Our technicians are certified and equipped to handle BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Lexus, Porsche, Land Rover, and other premium brands with specialized programming protocols.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. Payment is collected upon successful completion of the service.
