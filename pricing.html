FAQ

Pricing Questions

Do you charge extra for luxury vehicles?
Some luxury brands require specialized programming protocols and proprietary equipment. A modest surcharge may apply for BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Porsche, Land Rover, and similar premium makes.
Is there a discount for multiple keys?
Yes. When you order a spare key along with your primary replacement, the additional key is discounted by . Fleet and multi-vehicle discounts are also available for commercial clients.
What if the key doesn't work after programming?
We guarantee all programming on vehicles we service. If the key doesn't function as expected, we'll troubleshoot and resolve the issue at no additional cost. You only pay when the key works correctly.
Do you accept insurance claims?
Yes. Many roadside assistance plans and comprehensive auto insurance policies cover key replacement. We can provide an itemized receipt for reimbursement. Check with your provider for coverage details.
