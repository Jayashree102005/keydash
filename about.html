Who We Are

More Than Just A Locksmith — We're Your Roadside Partner

Founded in 2009, Mobile Keys started as a small operation with a single van and a mission: make car key replacement fast, fair, and mobile. Today, we operate a fleet of fully-equipped service vehicles staffed by certified automotive locksmiths.

We've helped over 5,000 drivers across the metro area with key cutting, transponder programming, smart fob replacement, and emergency lockout services. Every job is backed by our satisfaction guarantee.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

Key moments that shaped Mobile Keys into the trusted service provider it is today.

2009

The Beginning

Founded with a single mobile van and a vision to make car key replacement accessible to everyone.

2013

Transponder Expertise

Invested in advanced transponder programming equipment, expanding services to chipped keys and immobilizer systems.

2017

Fleet Expansion

Grew to a fleet of 5 mobile units, reducing average response time to under 30 minutes across the metro area.

2021

Smart Key Revolution

Became certified specialists in proximity fobs, push-to-start systems, and luxury vehicle smart key programming.

2024

Digital Dashboard Launch

Launched the customer portal with real-time technician tracking, digital quotes, and secure key code storage.
